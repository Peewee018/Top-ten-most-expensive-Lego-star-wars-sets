These are the Top Ten most exspensive lego sets

These sets are some of the most sought after sets in the world and if you get your hands on one of the sealed and in goo condition consider yourself extreamly lucky.



Number Ten

Life cycle 2001-2003

Rebel Blockade Runner - UCS: At $1,697.05, this set lets you construct the Tantive IV, the Rebel Blockade Runner seen at the beginning of “A New Hope.” It’s part of the UCS line.

Number Nine

Life cycle may 2019-june 2019

Resistance Bomber (Finch Dallow version): This set, priced at $1,940.70, lets you build the Resistance Bomber from “The Last Jedi.” It includes minifigures like Poe Dameron and Vice Admiral Holdo.

Number Eight

Only at the international toy fair 2005

Darth Vader: 56. International Toy Fair: Valued at $2,366.26, this exclusive set was released at the International Toy Fair. It features a large-scale Darth Vader minifigure.

Number Seven

Life cycle 2007-2010

Millennium Falcon - UCS: The iconic Millennium Falcon, priced at $2,377.88, is a massive set with incredible detail. It includes minifigures like Han Solo, Chewbacca, and Princess Leia.Resistance Bomber (Finch Dallow version): This set, priced at $1,640.70, lets you build the Resistance Bomber from “The Last Jedi.” It includes minifigures like Poe Dameron and Vice Admiral Holdo.

Number Six

Life cycle 2009-2011

Republic Dropship with AT-OT: Priced at $2,410.58, this set includes a Republic Dropship and an AT-OT walker. It’s perfect for recreating battle scenes from the Clone Wars.

Number Five

Life cycle 2002-2002

It’s a limited edition collectible.Naboo Starfighter - UCS: At $2,466.11, this set showcases the sleek Naboo N-1 Starfighter. It’s part of the Ultimate Collector Series (UCS) and includes a display stand.

Number Four

Life cycle 2007

Gold chrome plated C-3PO: Valued at $2,500.00, this unique set features a shiny gold C-3PO minifigure.

Number Three

Life cycle 2005-2007

Death Star II: Released at $3,067.54, this set allows you to construct the second Death Star from “Return of the Jedi.” It includes minifigures like Emperor Palpatine, Darth Vader, and Luke Skywalker.

Number Two

Life cycle 2001-2001

The LEGO 10018 Darth Maul Bust is a remarkable Star Wars set released in 2001. It features an intricately detailed Darth Maul bust, standing 17 inches tall. The set comprises 1,871 pieces, and its total weight is approximately 8.17 lbs. The set includes instructions but does not come with any minifigures12. If you’re a fan of Star Wars and LEGO, this collectible piece is a must-have for your collection.

Number One

Life cycle 2003-2005

Cloud City: This set, priced at $8,362.86, is a collector’s dream. It features iconic scenes from “The Empire Strikes Back,” including the carbon-freezing chamber and the dining room where Darth Vader reveals his true identity to Luke Skywalker.
